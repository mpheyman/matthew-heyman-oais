# Change Management (v0)

## When required
A Change Proposal is required for any Tier A modification.

## Change Proposal format
Create: features/<name>/change_proposal.md containing:

- Summary of change
- Why change is needed
- Impacted docs / components
- Risks
- Rollback plan
- Human decision (Approved/Rejected) + date

## Rule
No Tier A edits without an approved proposal.
