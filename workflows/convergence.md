# Convergence (v0)

Convergence is the human-in-the-loop quality gate that turns agent output into shippable product.

## Required inputs to converge
- What changed (diff summary)
- Why it changed (goal)
- How to validate (tests / manual checks)
- Risks (security, perf, regressions)
- Rollback plan (if applicable)

## Convergence outcome
- Approved to deploy
- Returned for iteration
- Rejected / killed
